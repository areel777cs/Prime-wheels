Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I rent a car?

You can rent a car by visiting our website, choosing your desired car, and completing the booking process.

2. What documents are required?

You need a valid driver's license, ID card or passport, and a credit card for the deposit.

3. Are there any age restrictions?

Yes, the minimum age to rent a car is 21 years. Some car types may require drivers to be at least 25 years old.

4. Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time without any charges.
